# Design system direction

## Palette
- Obsidian: `#0A0A10`
- Warm ivory: `#FAF8F5`
- Champagne gold: `#C9A84C`
- Supporting graphite and deep blue may be used at low saturation.

## Principles
- Strong hierarchy and generous negative space.
- Photography is part of the composition, not decoration.
- Gold is an accent, never a dominant fill across the page.
- Glass surfaces must remain readable and subtle.
- Motion is slow, deliberate, and cinematic.

## Avoid
Acid neon, excessive glow, random particles, bounce, flip, aggressive zoom, tiny text, noisy gradients, and effects that reduce contrast.

## Interaction
Hover states should be noticeable but restrained. Mobile layouts must not depend on hover. All motion must respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
