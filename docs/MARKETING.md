# Marketing and copy direction

## Core proposition
Strong speech turns knowledge, ideas, and experience into influence.

## Copy rules
Use concrete situations and observable outcomes. Prefer plain, confident Russian. Avoid empty superlatives, generic promises, bureaucratic language, and pressure tactics.

## Section jobs
- Hero: clarify the result and invite the next step.
- Philosophy: explain why delivery determines whether ideas are heard.
- Competencies: show practical transformation.
- Expert: build trust with facts and proof.
- Method: show a structured process.
- Speech Lab: create self-recognition and engagement.
- Form: reduce uncertainty and explain what happens after submission.
